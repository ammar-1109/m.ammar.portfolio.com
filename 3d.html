3D Printing – 7-Day Learning Module

Day 1

BasicsFDM

Learned what 3D printing is, types of 3D printing (focus on FDM), and printer parts: frame, bed, extruder, nozzle, filament, stepper motors, control board.

Day 2

MaterialsSafety

Studied filaments (PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU), bed adhesion methods, and safety precautions for handling hot nozzle and moving parts.

Day 3

CADSTL

Learned basics of 3D design software (TinkerCAD/Fusion360), understood STL file format, and practiced downloading models from Thingiverse.

Day 4

SlicerSettings

Installed Cura/PrusaSlicer, learned key slicing settings: layer height, infill, supports, speed & temperature, and previewed toolpaths.

Day 5

CalibrationFirst Print

Learned bed leveling, nozzle height adjustments, and printed first test objects (calibration cube, Benchy), observing common printing issues.

Day 6

TroubleshootingPrint Quality

Identified and fixed printing problems: bed adhesion, stringing, layer shifting, and experimented with settings to improve print quality.

Day 7

Post-processingAdvanced Learning

Learned post-processing: sanding, painting, acetone smoothing, explored advanced filaments, SLA/DLP printing, and printed useful objects like phone stands and keychains.
